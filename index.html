ABOUT ME

I have dogs myself, and I go on walks with them everyday in the countryside. All our dogs sleep indoors, so no one is left out, including yours! Why does my service stand out...? I live in the countryside, with a big garden, where there's loads of space for the dogs to run around and play. I own dogs myself... So your dog will be socialised , and of course have tons of new playmates! Your dog will get to go on 3 hour nature walks everyday (2 walks a day) Our favourite places to walk are the forest and bog. At the end of the day your dog will get to sleep inside, on a nice comfy couch, or bed, (whatever he prefers) Basically he will be busy having his own mini holiday, so no sad goodbyes! I also offer pick up and drop off services, and keep the owners updated by sending photos and videos every day.
